About Me

Hello, I'm Vinh Nguyen, a Computer Science student at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities. I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science and a Master of Science degree at this institution.

My interests are in the area of Computer Systems, where I shoot myself in the foot while programming in C. I enjoy C's simplicity (lol) and how closely I can interact with the hardware compared to other languages. I am also decently skilled at C++, and recently I have started to program in Rust. With these tools, I make computer programs for myself to use. While I write these programs to learn new things and to hone my skills, I end up using them quite a lot due to how minimal their interfaces are. You can say I'm quite a digital minimalist.

Despite being a "digital minimalist", I am extremely passionate about a wide array of topics within Computer Science. While my main focus is on Computer Systems and low-level programming, I also enjoy learning and taking on other interesting projects such as creating web pages like this one, training a specialized Machine Learning model, and studying the best practices of Software Engineering.
